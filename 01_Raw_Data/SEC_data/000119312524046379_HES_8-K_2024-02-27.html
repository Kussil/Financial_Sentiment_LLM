Item 8.01

Other Events

As previously disclosed, on October 22, 2023, Hess Corporation (“Hess”), Chevron Corporation (“Chevron”) and Yankee Merger Sub Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Chevron (“Merger Sub”), entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger, pursuant to which, among other things and subject to the terms and conditions therein, Merger Sub will be merged with and into Hess, with Hess surviving the merger as a direct, wholly owned subsidiary of Chevron (such transaction, the “Merger”).

Hess is filing this Current Report on Form 8-K to ensure that its investors and other interested parties are aware that on February 26, 2024, Chevron filed a registration statement on Form S-4 (the “Form S-4”) with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission containing a preliminary prospectus of Chevron that also constitutes a preliminary proxy statement of Hess (the “Preliminary Proxy Statement/Prospectus”) with respect to the Merger.

The Preliminary Proxy Statement/Prospectus will be updated and revised by Chevron and Hess prior to becoming definitive and being mailed to Hess stockholders, and Hess undertakes no obligation, and does not intend, to file additional updates regarding the status of the Preliminary Proxy Statement/Prospectus or the information set forth therein, except for a definitive proxy statement on Schedule 14A following effectiveness of the Form S-4.
