Item 8.01.  Other Events.

On April 24, 2019, Occidental Petroleum Corporation (“Occidental”) issued a press release (the “Press Release”) announcing its proposal (the “Proposal”) to acquire Anadarko Petroleum Corporation (“Anadarko”) for $76.00 per Anadarko share, based on Occidental’s closing price on April 23, 2019.  Under the terms of the Proposal, Anadarko shareholders would receive $38.00 in cash and 0.6094 shares of Occidental common stock for each share of Anadarko common stock.

The foregoing description of the Proposal is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Press Release, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
