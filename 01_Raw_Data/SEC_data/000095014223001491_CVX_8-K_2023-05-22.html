Item 8.01Other Events.

On May 22, 2023, Chevron Corporation (“Chevron”) and PDC Energy, Inc. (“PDC”) issued a news release announcing the execution of an Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of May 21, 2023, by and among Chevron, PDC, and Bronco Merger Sub Inc., a direct, wholly-owned subsidiary of Chevron. A copy of the news release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference.
