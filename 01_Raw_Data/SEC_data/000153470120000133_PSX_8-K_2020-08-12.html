Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
On August 12, 2020, Phillips 66 (the “Company”) issued a news release announcing plans to reconfigure its San Francisco Refinery in Rodeo, California, to produce renewable fuels. In connection with the issuance of the news release, the Company posted to its website presentation slides related to the project that may be used by management in discussions with investors.
A copy of the Company’s press release and the related presentation slides are being furnished as Exhibit 99.1 and Exhibit 99.2, respectively, to this Current Report on Form 8-K and are also available on the Company’s website at www.phillips66.com.
The information contained in this Item 7.01, including Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, is being furnished and shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of Section 18, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing.
