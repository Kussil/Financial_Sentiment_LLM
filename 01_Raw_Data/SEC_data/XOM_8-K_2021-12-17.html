Item 1.02   Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

The information set forth in Item 8.01 below as to the satisfaction and discharge of the Indenture dated as of March 20, 2014 (as amended or supplemented to date, the “Indenture”) between Exxon Mobil Corporation (the “Company”) and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as trustee (the “Trustee”) with respect to the Notes (as defined below) is incorporated by reference into this Item 1.02.

 

Item 2.04  

Triggering Events That Accelerate or Increase a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement.

 

On December 16, 2021, at the Company’s request, the Trustee delivered a notice of redemption to holders of the Company’s 2.397% Notes due 2022 issued under the Indenture (the “Notes”), stating that the Company will redeem all $1,150.0 million in aggregate principal amount of the outstanding Notes, in accordance with the Indenture. The redemption date will be January 6, 2022 (the “Redemption Date”) and the redemption price will be 100.000% of the principal amount of the Notes redeemed plus accrued and unpaid interest thereon to, but not including, the Redemption Date. No 2.397% Notes due 2022 will remain outstanding.

 

Item 8.01   Other Events.

 

On December 17, 2021, the Company irrevocably deposited with the Trustee sufficient funds to fund the redemption of the outstanding Notes on the Redemption Date. After the deposit of such funds, the Indenture was satisfied and discharged with respect to the Notes in accordance with its terms. The Company paid for the discharge of all such Notes using cash on hand.

 

As a result of the satisfaction and discharge of the Indenture with respect to the Notes, the Company has been released from its obligations under the Indenture in respect of the Notes, except those provisions of the Indenture that, by their terms, survive the satisfaction and discharge of the Indenture.
